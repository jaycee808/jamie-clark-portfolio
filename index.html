02
about me.

Hello, my name is Jamie. I'm a passionate Junior Frontend Web Developer based in Manchester, UK. I recently completed edX's rigorous Frontend Web Development Bootcamp, which has given me a comprehensive understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, UI/UX, Git, GitHub, API's, and React.

I'm excited to bring my strong work ethic and exceptional organisation skills to an entry-level web developer position. My goal is to contribute my skills to a dynamic team that values creativity, innovation, and problem-solving.

Web development has allowed me to explore my creativity and use my curiosity to solve problems. I'm excited to continue learning and growing in the field of web development while pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Outside of work, I'm an avid traveller who loves exploring new cultures and cuisines. I'm also a huge fan of music, true-crime documentaries, horror films, and trash television.
